About University Of Balochistan

The University of Balochistan (UoB), located in Quetta, is the oldest and largest public university in the province of Balochistan, Pakistan. Established in October 1970, it started with only three departments—Physics, Chemistry, and Geology—but has since grown into a major institution with over 50 departments across 6 faculties, offering BS, M.Phil, and Ph.D. programs. The university is governed by the Governor of Balochistan as its Chancellor, and Prof. Dr. Zahoor Ahmed Bazai currently serves as the Vice Chancellor.

Our mission is to foster intellectual curiosity, empower individuals to realize their fullest potential, and contribute meaningfully to the betterment of society. commitment to academic excellence, diversity, and community engagement.
