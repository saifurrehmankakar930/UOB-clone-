The history of University Of Balochistan

The University of Balochistan (UoB) was established in 1970 in Quetta, the capital of Balochistan, Pakistan. It is the oldest and largest higher education institution in the province. The university was created by an ordinance passed by the Government of Balochistan, with the aim of providing quality education to the people of the region and promoting research and academic development. Initially, the university began with three departments: Physics, Chemistry, and Geology. Over the decades, it has grown into a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields such as social sciences, natural sciences, languages, education, law, and management sciences.
